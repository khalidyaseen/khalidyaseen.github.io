About JKFA

The J&K Football Association was founded in 1964 in order to develop and harness the footballing talent in the region. In the same year of its existence it was granted affiliation by All India Football Federation (AIFF) and J&K Sports Council. The J&K Football Association (JKFA) is the premier body for conducting and assisting football competitions and events in the J&K U.T. The JKFA Vision

By bringing the football experience closer, we bring sport itself closer to all; from this affinity, we will create an environment rich in enjoyment and happiness. By strengthening the base of football in J&K, we will create world-class players that will move, inspire and encourage the people of J&K. We will always act in a spirit of fair play, fostering friendships among the people in J&K and beyond, to contribute to the betterment of the whole community.

WHAT WE FOCUS ON

We Focus on making Football back to its charm at all levels. For that we are working on grass root level. Kashmir has very much hidden talent and we are trying to flourish the same.
